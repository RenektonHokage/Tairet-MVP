## Closed contract

- RPC work is bounded by abortable deadlines.
- The configured RPC/provider timeout plus a fixed safety margin must fit inside the fulfillment lease.
- A conclusive claim establishes a conservative local lease deadline.
- The reconcile-only worker processes issuance work and defers email work by a fenced release; it does not send durable email.
- Fatal clock/budget boundaries stop new authority-bearing work.

## Introduced invariants

- A stale, expired, or exhausted lease never regains mutation authority.
- Late responses after timeout or fatal stop are inert.
- Monotonic-clock failure latches a fatal stop.
- External shutdown releases safely claimable work without inventing authority.
- Logging failure cannot break fencing or cleanup.
